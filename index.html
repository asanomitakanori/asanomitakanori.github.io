Profile

I'm Master student in Kyushu University.
I received my Bachelor degree in Computer Science at the University of Kyushu in 2021.
I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Ryoma Bise.
During my Bachelor and Master, I worked in the intersection of machine learning, optimization and statistics.
My research centered around developing novel computationally efficient methods with theoretical guarantees for challenging machine learning problems, with an emphasis on finding exact solutions for nonconvex problems.
