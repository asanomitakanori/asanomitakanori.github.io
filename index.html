About

  • I'm Master student in Kyushu University. My current research focuses on generative models and unsupervised learning.
  • I received my Bachelor degree in Computer Science at the University of Kyushu in 2021. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Ryoma Bise.

    During my Bachelor and Master, I worked in the intersection of machine learning, optimization and statistics. My research centered around developing novel computationally efficient methods with theoretical guarantees for challenging machine learning problems.
  • I worked as a postdoc researcher in the Statistics Department, Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania.
